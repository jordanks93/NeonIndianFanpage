Bio

Neon Indian is an American electronic music band from Denton, Texas. The music is composed by Mexican-born Alan Palomo. The band's debut studio album, Psychic Chasms, was released in October 2009. Rolling Stone named Neon Indian one of the best new bands of 2010.
